In order to track your eligibility based on your mainnet validator's activity, we need to have proof that the team operating the mainnet validator is the same team operating the testnet validator (otherwise someone could claim to represent your mainnet validator and earn your rewards)!

On mainnet Cosmos Hub, send a trivial amount of ATOM (e.g. 0.001 uatom) from your mainnet validator address to the multisig address cosmos1078gmgntta9qguve7c52dhxrs45xumjvcwrutd. The memo field of this transaction must contain the cosmosvaloper address of your testnet validator.

For example:

  • Mainnet address is ABC.
  • Testnet address is XYZ.
  • From the cosmos wallet ABC, send us a tx with XYZ in the memo.
If doing so is against your key management rules, contact Lexa to discuss alternatives.
