Hypha's mandate

Our mandate on the Cosmos Hub is to:
  1. Steward the Hub's testnet as a platform for validator education, final runway for Hub upgrades, and stable development platform
  2. Provide mainnet and Interchain Security coordination support for validators and consumer chains
  3. Provide development support for the Hub team at Interchain Labs
We are open to feedback about our work, especially from the communities we serve directly: Hub validators, testnet validators, consumer chains, and Hub developers. We will address all feedback in our quarterly reviews, posted to the Hub forum.
