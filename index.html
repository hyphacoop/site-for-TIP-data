How does TIP work?

Each month, we run several events to help validators learn about gaia-specific features and improve their skill as node operators.

Validators on the Hub testnet register as testnet participants by providing us proof that their testnet validator is associated with a mainnet Hub validator using our Registration Form. If either your mainnet or testnet address changes, you must resubmit this Registration Form. Without this information, we cannot track your participation and you cannot earn points!

Points and uptime on the `provider` chain are reported to the Interchain Foundation on a quarterly basis to inform decisions in their ATOM delegations program.
